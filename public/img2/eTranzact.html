About Us
eTranzact is Nigeria's first award winning multi-application, multi-channel electronic transaction switching and payment processing platform. eTranzact handles billions of dollars yearly, supporting millions of forward thinking businesses and individuals who trust that their financial transactions would be taken care of by eTranzact daily.

Pioneers & Innovators in the African Payment System, since birth
2003

eTranzact opens its doors! Officially launching its proprietary payment Switch in 2003, eTranzact rolled out its operations.

2004

Diversifying its portfolio in 2004, eTranzact added banking Java Apps and ESA to its product suite.

2005

In 2005, the company officially released its enterprise bulk payment solution, CorporatePay. In the same year, it launched its patented cardless withdrawal product, CardLex.

2007

A crowning point, the company celebrated 1 million transactions across its platform of product.

2008

In 2008, eTranzact attained a great feat processing 10,000% more transactions within a year and reaching 1 billion transactions. She also celebrated her CardLex patent approval and introduced ESA to the market.

2009

eTranzact became public in 2009 and got listed on the Nigerian Stock Exchange. In the same year, expanding its product portfolio, the industry witnessed the invent of its USSD payment solution, as well as introductions of its airtime solution, JustTopUp and card processing gateway, WebConnect.

2010

Maintaining its streak of innovations, PocketMoni (a mobile wallet solution) was released in 2010, and the company broadened its processing capacity as MasterCard processor.

2011

Extending payment touch points to bank branches, eTranzact launched branch solution in 2011. Same year, eTranzact received its PCI-DSS certification, an attestation to its security standard compliance.

2013

eTranzact obtained her ISO 27001 certification, evident of her applied security controls and practices.

2015

2015 saw more novel products added to the eTranzact product mix. BankIT (a direct debit payment gateway service) and SwitchIT (all-inclusive API that powers your live switch) were inaugurated.

2016

New Wine. New Look. eTranzact refreshes her brand.

2019

eTranzact applies an organization-wide transformation people, process and technology.
